About Me

I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at NIT Delhi. My research primarily focuses on intelligent transportation systems, with a special interest in deep learning-based traffic flow prediction and road safety analytics.

Teaching

I am passionate about mentoring and teaching. I currently teach subjects related to Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. My teaching philosophy centers on active learning and practical exposure to real-world datasets and systems.
