About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at NIT Delhi. I completed my PhD from IIT Roorkee, where I worked on attention-based deep learning models for traffic flow prediction. My academic journey has been driven by a passion to solve real-world transportation problems using intelligent systems.

Research Interests

  • Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)
  • Traffic Flow Prediction using Deep Learning
  • Spatio-temporal Modeling of Road Networks
  • Road Safety and Anomaly Detection
  • Data-driven Solutions for Indian Traffic Challenges

Research Opportunities

I welcome motivated undergraduate and postgraduate students to join my research group. You can work on real-world problems such as:

  • Accident Detection and Alert Systems
  • Traffic Anomaly Identification using Computer Vision
  • Predictive Modeling for Congestion Hotspots

If you're interested, please email me with your resume and area of interest.
